About

W.R. Dunkin and Son is a builder striving to provide quality service to each client. New ideas and the ability to remain fast and flexible in this traditional construction process, allows the W.R. Dunkin team to remain competitive in every aspect. We provide exceptional value for each client by utilizing new and innovative solutions that meet their needs.

The W.R. Dunkin and Son company strives to be strategic partners in the construction process, not just contractors. We believe in a collaborative approach with the customer and architect to work as a team in setting and achieving goals for each project. With a commitment to excellence and precision, our responsive and skilled employees are proud of the long history of success that proceeds them. They look forward to a bright future, and are dedicated to building on a foundation that was firmly put in place over 90 years ago.

About

Formed in 2008 in Toronto, Ontario, the band consists of songwriter Simon Ward on vocals and guitar, David Ritter on vocals and keys, Jon Hembrey on lead guitar, Isabel Ritchie on violin, Darryl James on bass guitar, and Jeremy Drury on drums. Hembrey, James, Drury and Ward are all originally from Lindsay, Ontario, while Ritter and Ritchie joined after Ward posted a call for additional musicians to Craigslist.
